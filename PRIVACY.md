# Privacy Notice

Built by Alex Teplov, CPA. Effective date: May 2026.

---

## What This Repository Does

These workflows run inside your AI agent or assistant. They organize information you provide during the session. They do not collect, store, or transmit your data to Alex Teplov CPA or any third party on their own.

---

## What Data You Enter

When you use these workflows, you may enter personal and financial information such as:

- income amounts and sources
- business expenses
- GST/HST amounts
- tax instalment records
- foreign income details

This information stays within your AI session unless you choose to share it.

---

## Your AI Provider

These workflows run on the AI platform you choose (such as Claude, ChatGPT, or another agent). Your data passes through that platform under their own privacy policy and terms of service. You should review your AI provider's privacy policy before entering sensitive financial information.

**Do not enter your SIN, business number, bank account numbers, or full client names.** Redact sensitive identifiers before uploading any documents.

## When You Submit a Package to Teplov CPA

If you use the CPA handoff output to send a package to Alex Teplov CPA, the following applies:

- Your information is used only to provide the professional services you requested.
- It is retained in accordance with CPA professional standards and applicable law.
- It is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties except as required to deliver the service or comply with legal obligations.
- You may request access to or correction of your information at any time.

Submissions are governed by the engagement terms provided at the time of service.
